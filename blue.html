Project Overview

I've worked closely with the BLUE team of developers and the founder Butcher and I did develop in different aspects of the project, such as: developing a script to fetch real-time data from the blockchain to display current circulating supply (in the same way I did for $CAPO), a lottery game at lottery.profitblue.fun, helped to develop a script to airdrop CAPO to BLUE holders, the same described in the CAPO project.

- I've developed a staking website to earn $CAPO by staking $BLUE token, this staking dApp is not currently live but already fully developed in both back-end (smart contract) and front-end (react).
- I've also developed a script that takes the current holders of $CAPO by snapshot and by scraping the holder wallets and quantity of tokens, removes holders of 1% or above of supply, holders with less than 1 $CAPO token, and custom wallets to remove before running the script that are for example exchanges.
